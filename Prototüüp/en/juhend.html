Instructions

Fotoorienteerumine is a game, that combines a traditional orienteering, photo hunting and a quiz. Depending on the player, Fotoorienteerumine can be an intense sport or a fun entertainment exercise, that you can play alone or with friends. The goal of the game is to find the right spot on a landscape according to the map and find a detail that is on the given picture. When a player saves the right destination, 2 points will be added to his or her score and there is an opportunity to answer a multiple answer bonus question. If the answer is correct, the player will receive an extra point. If the answer is wrong, one point will be deducted from the players score. There is the option to forgo the question in which case the score will stay the same. The player has 30 seconds to answer the bonus question. After visiting all the destinations or choosing to stop the game, the point-score will be saved. The best scores will be shown on the games homepage.
For a comfortable game, use our phone app.
